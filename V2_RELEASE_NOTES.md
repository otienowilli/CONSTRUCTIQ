## 🚀 What's New in v2.0.0

### **Major Release: Complete Flutter Mobile App**

CONSTRUCTIQ v2.0 marks a complete transformation from a web-based prototype to a professional cross-platform mobile application built with Flutter. This release includes a comprehensive universal materials & machines system that works globally.

---

## ✨ New Features

### **📱 Complete Mobile App (25+ Screens)**

#### **Authentication & Onboarding**
- Beautiful animated splash screen
- 3-slide interactive onboarding
- Secure login & registration
- JWT-based authentication
- Role-based access control

#### **10 Advanced Material Calculators**
1. **Bitumen Calculator** - Road surface application rates with multiple grades
2. **Asphalt Calculator** - Hot mix asphalt weight & volume calculations
3. **Geotextile Calculator** - Ground stabilization with overlap calculations
4. **Concrete Calculator** - Complete material breakdown
5. **Brickwork Calculator** - Brick and mortar quantities
6. **Plastering Calculator** - Wall plastering estimation
7. **Painting Calculator** - Paint coverage with multiple coats
8. **Excavation Calculator** - Earthwork volume calculations
9. **Road Calculator** - Road construction materials
10. **Roofing Calculator** - Roofing material estimation

**Calculator Features:**
- Multiple material grades/types
- Wastage factor adjustments
- Real-time cost calculations
- Save & share results
- Unit conversions

#### **Universal Materials System**
- **80+ Pre-configured Materials** across 7 categories:
  - Cement & Binders (10)
  - Aggregates (10)
  - Bricks & Blocks (10)
  - Steel & Reinforcement (12)
  - Bitumen (10 grades)
  - Asphalt (9 types)
  - Geotextiles (8 types)
- **Add Custom Materials** - Unlimited user-defined materials
- Support for any material from any country

#### **Universal Machines System**
- **80+ Pre-configured Machines** across 8 categories:
  - Earthmoving Equipment (10)
  - Concrete Equipment (10)
  - Road Construction (11)
  - Compaction Equipment (5)
  - Crushing & Screening (6)
  - Material Handling (6)
  - Piling Equipment (4)
  - Generators & Compressors (5)
- **Add Custom Machines** - Support for any manufacturer
- Works with CAT, Komatsu, Volvo, Hitachi, JCB, and ANY brand

#### **Universal IoT Sensor System**
- **40+ Standard Sensor Codes**:
  - Location & Movement (GPS, Gyro, Accelerometer, Speed, Distance)
  - Environmental (Temperature, Humidity, Pressure, Vibration, Noise)
  - Soil & Material (Moisture, pH, Temperature, Density, Compaction)
  - Fluid & Flow (Water, Flow Meter, Fuel, Oil Pressure)
  - Load & Force (Weight, Torque, Strain)
  - Machine Status (Engine metrics, Battery, Hydraulic)
  - Safety (Emergency Stop, Collision, Tilt, Barrier)
- **Custom Sensor Support** - Add any sensor code
- **Universal Commands** - START, STOP, PAUSE, EMERGENCY STOP, etc.

#### **Project Management**
- **Project List** - View all projects with status indicators
- **Project Details** ⭐ NEW - 4 tabs (Overview, Tasks, Team, Budget)
- **Add Project** ⭐ NEW - Complete project creation form
- Progress tracking & budget management
- Team member assignment
- Task management
- Timeline visualization

#### **Machine Management**

#### **AI Features**

## 🏗️ Technical Improvements

### **Architecture**
- **Clean Architecture** - Separation of concerns (Models, Services, Providers, UI)
- **Provider Pattern** - Efficient state management
- **Offline-First** - SQLite local storage with cloud sync
- **Type-Safe** - Full Dart null safety
- **Scalable** - Easy to extend and maintain

### **Technology Stack**
- **Flutter** 3.24+ / Dart 3.5+
